# Physician Licensure Content Plan - 50 Articles

## Overview

This content plan covers 50 SEO-optimized articles targeting physician (MD/DO) licensure topics including:
- Medical license renewals and fees
- CME requirements and non-compliance consequences
- State-specific regulations and penalties
- IMLC/telemedicine licensing
- Disciplinary actions and reinstatement

**Target Audience**: Physicians (MD/DO), medical practice managers, hospital credentialing staff
**Content Governance**: L1.5 autonomy, max 3000 words, min SEO score 50
**Task ID Format**: `EDITORIAL-PHYSICIAN-{SUBCATEGORY}-{SEQ}`
